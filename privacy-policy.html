Information We Collect

The App does not require you to create an account and does not collect your name, email address, or any personally identifying information.

The App stores the following information locally on your device only:

Depending on your device and region, you may be able to limit ad tracking or personalized ads through your device's privacy settings.

You can review Google's privacy policy at https://policies.google.com/privacy and AdMob's policies at https://support.google.com/admob/answer/6128543.

In-App Purchases

The App offers optional in-app purchases, processed entirely by Apple or Google. We do not collect or store your payment information. Purchases are subject to Apple's or Google's own privacy policies and terms.

Children's Privacy

The App is intended for a general audience. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13. Since the App does not collect personal information from any user, this applies equally to all age groups.

Data Security

Because gameplay and settings data is stored only locally on your device, there is no server-side storage of your information for us to secure. Standard device-level security (such as your device passcode) protects this data.
